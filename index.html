  • Display immutable audit trail in case timeline.

  • Show escalated ticket with full context from bot/prior messages.

  • Outcome: No free typing, all fields pre-populated, ACW near-zero.

  • Show live KPIs: QRT < 2 hours, bot deflection rate, 100% SOP adherence.
